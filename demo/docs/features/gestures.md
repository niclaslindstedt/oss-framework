# Cloud sync

Your lists live on your device first. Cloud sync keeps a copy in a backend you choose, so the same lists follow you to every device you sign in on — without ever needing an account on our side.

## How it works

When sync is on, every edit is saved locally the instant you make it, then queued for the backend. The status glyph in the list header tells you where that copy stands at a glance:

- A steady check means everything is saved.
- A spinning arrow means an upload is in flight.
- A warning means the last save did not land — open the details to retry.

Tapping the glyph opens the sync command centre. It walks you through the states that need a decision: reconnecting when your session expires, picking a side when the same list was changed in two places, and replaying edits you made while offline.

## Your data stays yours

Because the app is local-first, you can turn sync off at any time and keep working entirely on-device. Nothing is sent anywhere until you opt in, and the backend only ever holds the lists you chose to sync.
