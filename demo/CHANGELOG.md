# Changelog

All notable changes to the demo checklist app are documented here. The format
follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/).

## [1.4.0] - 2026-06-27

### Added

- **About menu** — a footer dropdown that folds away the project links (this
  "What's new" dialog and the source code), opening upward at the foot of the
  navigation drawer.
- **Donate** — a footer link for supporting the project.

## [1.3.0] - 2026-05-30

### Added

- **Workspaces** — group your lists into separate namespaces, each with its own
  glyph and accent colour; the browser tab re-badges to the active workspace.
- **Cloud sync** — a simulated backend with a header status glyph and a details
  command centre that walks you through reconnecting, conflicts, and offline
  edits. [Learn more](feature:gestures)

### Changed

- The navigation drawer now docks permanently as a sidebar on tablet-width and
  wider screens, and collapses to a draggable floating button on phones.

## [1.2.0] - 2026-04-18

### Added

- **Folders** — file your checklists into collapsible groups, reorder them by
  drag, and rename or archive a whole folder at once.
- **Achievements** — every feature you discover unlocks a trophy, sorted into
  four tiers from Beginner to Expert.

### Fixed

- Swipe-to-archive no longer fires when a horizontal scroll was intended.

## [1.1.0] - 2026-03-02

### Added

- **Search** — find any list or item across every workspace, with wildcard and
  regular-expression queries.
- **Themes** — pick a built-in look or mix your own colours, font, text size,
  and corner radius; the choice applies live.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-02-01

### Added

- The first release: a local-first nested-checklist PWA. Add lists and items,
  check them off, and watch the progress ring fill. Everything is stored on
  your device — there is no account and no server.
