`@niclaslindstedt/oss-framework/theme` — `SettingsModal`, a self-contained dialog over the theme module, and `AppearancePicker`, the controlled appearance editor it wraps (theme mode/variant, font, text size, and the Custom colour and shape/motion controls), plus a `DEFAULT_THEME_APPEARANCE` starting point; both drive a `ThemeAppearance` you apply live with `useApplyTheme`.
