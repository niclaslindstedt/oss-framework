---
type: Changed
title: Checklist drag-to-nest
---

`Checklist` reorder rebuilt as a finger-following lift with a dashed landing ghost and three drop zones — drag onto a row's edge to reorder, onto its middle to nest it as a sub-item (the ghost indents and shrinks to the child it will become while the target row is ringed), committed through `moveNode`'s new `"into"` mode.
