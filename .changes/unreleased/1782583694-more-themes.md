`theme`: added five distinct presets — **Tokyo Night** (deep indigo), **Catppuccin Mocha** (soft pastel lavender) and **Rosé Pine** (muted rose/plum) in the dark family, plus **Catppuccin Latte** (cool pastel) and **Rosé Pine Dawn** (warm rosy) in the light family. Each was contrast-checked so every body-text pair clears WCAG AA and every accent ≥3:1 (the pastel lights' accents are darkened from upstream to stay legible).
