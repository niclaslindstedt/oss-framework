type: Added
title: Swipe-to-delete rows
---

`hooks`: a `useRowSwipe` gesture hook — arm on a dominant horizontal drag, latch a trailing action open on a left swipe, or flick the row away on a right swipe — with configurable pixel thresholds. `checklist`: `Checklist` grows an optional `onDelete` (with a `deleteLabel`) that wires this gesture into each row's swipe-to-delete, plus a pure `removeNode(nodes, id)` tree helper.
