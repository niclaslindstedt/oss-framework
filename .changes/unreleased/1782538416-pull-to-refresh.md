type: Added
title: Pull-to-refresh gesture
---

A `usePullToRefresh` hook (in `/hooks`) drives a touch pull-to-refresh at the top of a scroll region — rubber-band damping, a four-state machine, and self-gating against modals, forms, and mid-scroll — and a matching `PullToRefreshIndicator` (in `/components`) renders the slide-down pill that surfaces it.
