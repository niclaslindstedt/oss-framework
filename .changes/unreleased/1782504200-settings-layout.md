title: Settings-layout building blocks
---

`components`: `Section`, `Field`, and `ToggleRow` — the presentational building blocks a settings surface is assembled from (a bordered group card, a labelled control row, and a checkbox+label+hint row). They arrange the framework's other primitives (`SegmentedControl`, `SelectPicker`, …) and carry no i18n or app domain; all visible strings inject as props.
